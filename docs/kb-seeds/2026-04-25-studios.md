# KB Seed Batch — London Studios (2026-04-25)

Studio + workspace category. Starting with Tradestars (3 London locations) under
their founder Roy Shaby. Will expand with Hackney Wick and other artist-studio
operators in subsequent appends.

**Tag convention:** `studio` is the canonical category tag. Spaces that also offer
coworking get both `studio` and `coworking`.

**Sources:**
- Tradestars launch coverage: https://www.london-now.co.uk/news/25784841.tradestars-opens-doors-former-alexander-mcqueen-hq/
- Tradestars locations cross-referenced via tagvenue, vibewarehouses, hubblehq listings (the tradestars.space site itself sits behind Cloudflare and 403s curl/WebFetch).
- Roy Shaby: https://www.linkedin.com/in/roy-shaby/ + https://realbusiness.co.uk/3k-acquisition-uber-co-founder-roy-shaby-talks-lessons-newest-venture-tradestars

**Sources used (per entity):**
- **tradestars-islington** — 4-10 North Road, Holloway N7. Geocode `4-10 North Road London N7` → 51.5493308, -0.1211502 ("Delta House, 4-10 North Road, Holloway"). Former Alexander McQueen HQ.
- **tradestars-hackney-wick** — 415 Wick Lane, E3 2JG. Geocode `415 Wick Lane London E3` → 51.5364024, -0.0225302. Studios from 150–1000 sq ft, from £950.
- **tradestars-southwark** — 32-36 Loman Street, SE1 0EH. Geocode → 51.5029527, -0.0997699. Near London Bridge.
- **roy-shaby** — Founder/CEO of Tradestars. Serial entrepreneur (founded Running Sushi, sold a dark-kitchen biz to Travis Kalanick).
